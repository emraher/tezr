# tezr: Access the Turkiye's National Thesis Center Database

Access the Turkiye's National Thesis Center (Ulusal Tez Merkezi)
database at <https://tez.yok.gov.tr>. Search, retrieve, and analyze
dissertation metadata from Turkish universities. Supports basic and
advanced search, thesis details retrieval, lookup tables, and aggregate
statistics.
